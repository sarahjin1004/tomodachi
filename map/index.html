Worldwide Conventions Map

Welcome to the worldwide conventions map, a map of all the fan conventions we can find in the world. Zoom into an area near you to see a bunch of awesome conventions in your area. We make no guarantees as to the accuracy of information contained here, so please verify information before using it.

It is our goal to bring you the most complete list of fan conventions anywhere in the world. Whether you like anime, comic books, science fiction, video games, or popular culture, we hope to include it here. We want you to have the chance to find exciting conventions to attend and cosplay at. Go out and have fun making the world a better place by spreading geek fandom and culture.

The UpcomingCons map is developed by Ryan Kopf. You can view more conventions on each category page, such as anime or comic cons. Also see state pages for: Texas, California, Illinois, Pennsylvania, Michigan, Washington, Tennesee, Wisconsin, Chicago, Ohio, Georgia, Kentucky, Alabama, Minnesota, Florida, Virginia, Maryland, Missouri, Virginia, Kansas, and Utah.
